Pizza

This pizza crust recipe was given to me by a friend. It is quick, easy, and delicious!

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Additional Time: 35 mins Total Time: 1 hr 10 mins Servings: 12 Yield: 1 large crust

Ingredients

  1. 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  2. 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  3. 2 cups bread flour, plus more for dusting
  4. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  5. 2 teaspoons white sugar
  6. 1 teaspoon salt
