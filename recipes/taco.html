Taco

This recipe is easy, yet delicious when used in tacos or other Mexican cuisine. Try it for enchiladas and nachos too.

Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 6 hrs Total Time: 6 hrs 5 mins Servings: 4 Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  1. 1 (1.25 ounce) package dry taco seasoning mix
  2. 1 cup chicken broth
  3. 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts
