Sushi

Homemade salmon roll made with sushi rice, nori, and smoked salmon. This is a very basic and easy recipe for making sushi. It might take a while to learn how to make a perfect roll, but you'll have fun trying! Add wasabi (Japanese horseradish) for a good kick!

Prep Time: 30 mins Additional Time: 4 hrs 30 mins Total Time: 5 hrs Servings: 6 Yield: 6 salmon rolls

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups Japanese sushi rice
  2. 6 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
  3. 6 sheets nori (dry seaweed)
  4. 2 tablespoons wasabi paste
  5. 8 ounces smoked salmon, cut into long strips
  6. 1 cucumber, peeled and sliced
  7. 1 avocado - peeled, pitted and sliced
